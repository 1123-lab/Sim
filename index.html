About: Home

Welcome to the Nautical Incident Lab, your central hub for maritime safety training. From here, you can access all available simulation scenarios, track your progress, and manage your training sessions. The dashboard provides an overview of completed simulations, performance statistics, and recommendations for further training based on your current skill level and areas that need improvement.

Expand your maritime safety expertise with our premium simulation library. This Pro feature unlocks access to additional incident scenarios covering various vessel types, emergency situations, and maritime environments. About: Nautical Incidents Lab

The Nautical Incidents Lab is an advanced maritime safety training platform designed to prepare maritime professionals for real-world emergency situations. Our simulations are based on actual maritime incidents that have occurred over the past decades, with details carefully modified for educational purposes. The platform was developed in collaboration with maritime safety experts, ship captains, and emergency response specialists to ensure authentic and effective training experiences that can save lives at sea.

About: Incident on 'Agatha'

The simulation you are about to start is based on a real incident that unfolded 20 years ago, on the real vessel with the same number of crew aboard, but its details altered for the purpose of this simulation.

You are the Captain of the vessel 'Agatha'. Your objective is to navigate through the nine phases of a developing emergency. At each phase, you will be presented with a scenario and three possible decisions. Choose the action you believe is most effective. Your performance will be evaluated based on the consequences of your choices. Your final score will account for the number of correct decisions made, the total time spent, and, most importantly, preventing crew and external casualties.

О программе: Инцидент на 'Агате'

Эта симуляция основана на реальном инциденте, который произошел 20 лет назад. Вы возьмете на себя роль капитана жилого судна 'Агата' во время развивающейся чрезвычайной ситуации.

Ваша цель - пройти девять фаз инцидента, принимая наиболее эффективные решения на каждом этапе. Ваша работа будет оцениваться по правильности решений, управлению временем и, что самое важное, безопасности вашей команды и предотвращению внешних жертв. Обратите пристальное внимание на предоставленные детали, так как они критически важны для успешного исхода.
